Resources

This list is not an exhaustive list but I want to keep track of books, podcasts, and websites that I have found helpful.

Podcasts
  • Raw Data by P3 Adaptive
    • As soon as I hear the familiar "Hello Friends" it makes we feel like I am among friends discussing topics especially with how it impacts the business
  • SQL Data Partners
  • Data Driven
  • RunAsRadio
  • Power Query Magic
  • Kasper On BI
  • BIFocal - Clarifying Business Intelligence
Books
  • Definitive Guide to DAX - Second Edition
  • PowerPivot Book
  • Master Your Data with Excel and Power BI
Websites
  • SQLBI
    • Great blog posts as well as video courses. Great value and they keep updating the courses and provide the updated course at no additional price.
  • P3 Adaptive (Formerly Power Pivot Pro)
    • So many good blog posts over the years. To me the green stick figure drawings are iconic in breaking down concepts into simpler explanations. A treasure trove of information and especially business focused.
  • Radacad
    • Books and blog posts on Power BI.
  • Brent Ozar
    • Everything from learning to be a better DBA to an informative newsletter with great links
Tools
  • Model Auto Build
    • Go for details on Model Auto Build but stay for all the other great content on his site
  • DAX.do
    • Great place to practice DAX code. Can also share code and has built in formatter.
  • DAX Formatter
    • Website that will format your DAX code and even has options for long lines, short lines, etc.
